Daily Stocks and Prices: Minor Metals

The LME publishes daily official prices (both bid and offer) for cobalt and molybdenum traded on the Exchange. The Official Prices established are basis ‘open-outcry’ trading at the end of the first Ring session of the day. The Settlement Price is the official cash seller’s price (offer).

Minor Metals contracts commenced trading on the Ring on 22nd February 2010 with futures prompt dates from 3 months. During this launch period Cash Buyer and Cash Seller & Settlement prices are not available until the Cash Prompt Date becomes tradable.

Check the minor metals section of lme.com for more information on the launch.

All LME prices are reported in US Dollars per metric tonne.
